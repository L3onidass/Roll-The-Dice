Welcome to the game Roll The Dice!

The rules are simple. Roll the dice and try to collect 100 points before the opponent. If you roll 1, you will lose all current points and turn. For secure collected current points against loss use save score button, but you pass the game to the other player.

Press R button in the right corner to close the window.

Wish you good luck and have a fun!
